Comparisons

Comparisons are generated by the bassist. They are diffs between a base system and an observed system. The diffs from bassist comparisons are gathered into sets. Click here to assign comparison diffs to sets.

Sets

Sets are diffs that are gathered together for a purpose. For example: in configuration management you might specify "install package apache", which installs several packages, installs a new user or group, installs a service, and starts a system process. Click here to view and edit the diffs within sets.
